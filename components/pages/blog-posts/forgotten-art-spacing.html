Why Spacing Matters

Proper spacing is one of the most fundamental yet often overlooked aspects of good design. It affects readability, hierarchy, and overall user experience.

Types of Spacing

  • Letter-spacing: Space between individual characters
  • Word-spacing: Space between words
  • Line-height: Vertical space between lines of text
  • Margins: Space around elements
  • Padding: Internal space within elements

Spacing Principles

Use consistent spacing scales, consider visual hierarchy, and always test your spacing decisions across different screen sizes and devices.
