Essential Typography Choices

Typography is the foundation of good design. The right font choice can make or break a design project. Here are some of the most reliable and versatile fonts that every designer should have in their toolkit.

Serif Fonts

  • Georgia: Excellent for web readability
  • Times New Roman: Classic and professional
  • Merriweather: Modern serif with great legibility

Sans-Serif Fonts

  • Helvetica: Clean and versatile
  • Arial: Web-safe and readable
  • Open Sans: Friendly and modern
  • Roboto: Google's system font

Display Fonts

  • Playfair Display: Elegant and sophisticated
  • Montserrat: Modern and geometric
  • Lobster: Fun and casual

When choosing fonts, consider the context, audience, and medium. A font that works well for print might not be ideal for digital interfaces, and vice versa.

Font Pairing Tips

The key to successful typography is pairing fonts that complement each other. Generally, pair a serif with a sans-serif, or use fonts from the same family with different weights.
